Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with my insurance company?

Yes. We have experience working with all major insurance carriers and can help with estimates, documentation, and communication so your claim moves forward smoothly.

Are estimates really free?

Yes — all written damage estimates are free, with no obligation.

What areas do you serve?

Our home base is Baton Rouge, Louisiana, and we serve clients across Louisiana, Texas, Mississippi, Alabama, and Florida.

Do you speak Spanish?

Sí. Atendemos a clientes en español. Just let us know your preferred language when you reach out.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, debit cards, and checks.

What are your hours?

Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Closed weekends. For emergencies, leave a message and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
