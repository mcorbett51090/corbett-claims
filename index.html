How It Works

Click any step to see what to expect at that stage.

1

Send the Request

An insurance carrier, attorney, or owner sends us the assignment details.

Send by phone, email, or the form below. We confirm receipt and gather any missing details within one business day. Photos and police reports are welcome but not required up front.
Details
2

On-Site Inspection

We inspect the vehicle or equipment in person and document the condition and damage.

Inspections are typically scheduled within 2–3 business days of assignment, sooner for rush work. We photograph all damage, measure where applicable, note pre-existing condition, and verify VIN / serial numbers on heavy equipment.
Details
3

Independent Report

You receive a detailed, impartial appraisal with photos and supporting valuations.

Written report typically delivered within 48 hours of inspection. Includes itemized damage estimate or total-loss valuation, comparable-vehicle research where relevant, and photos included.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who do you work for?

Insurance companies, attorneys, and private individuals. We're a fully independent appraiser — we don't repair vehicles, we don't sell parts, and we have no ties to any carrier.

What kinds of vehicles and equipment do you appraise?

Cars and light trucks, motorcycles, RVs and motorhomes (Class A, B, and C, plus trailers and fifth wheels), and heavy farm equipment such as tractors, combines, and implements.

What does an appraisal cost?

Fees depend on the assignment type, the equipment involved, and the travel required. Contact us with the details and we'll quote you up front.

How fast do you respond?

We respond to assignment requests within one business day. Inspections are typically scheduled within 2–3 business days, and written reports are delivered within 48 hours of inspection.

What areas do you serve?

We're headquartered in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, and regularly accept assignments across Louisiana, Texas, Mississippi, Alabama, Florida, Ohio, Illinois, Nevada, and Georgia — essentially from Texas to Florida and up to Ohio, Illinois, Nevada, and Georgia. Reach out and we'll confirm coverage for your location.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Check, Venmo, PayPal, Cash App, Apple Cash, and Zelle.

What are your hours?

Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Closed weekends. Leave a message after hours and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
