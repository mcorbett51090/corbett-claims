Frequently Asked Questions

Who do you work for?

Insurance companies, attorneys, and private individuals. We're a fully independent appraiser — we don't repair vehicles, we don't sell parts, and we have no ties to any carrier.

What kinds of vehicles and equipment do you appraise?

Cars and light trucks, motorcycles, RVs and motorhomes (Class A, B, and C, plus trailers and fifth wheels), and heavy farm equipment such as tractors, combines, and implements.

What does an appraisal cost?

Fees depend on the assignment type, the equipment involved, and the travel required. Contact us with the details and we'll quote you up front.

What areas do you serve?

We're headquartered in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, and regularly accept assignments across Louisiana, Texas, Florida, Ohio, Illinois, and Nevada — essentially from Texas to Florida and up to Ohio, Illinois, and Nevada. Reach out and we'll confirm coverage for your location.

How experienced is the appraiser?

Founder Ronnie Corbett spent 12 years as a staff adjuster at Allstate Insurance before starting Corbett Claims as an independent appraiser in 2001 — over 37 years of combined claims and appraisal experience.

Do you speak Spanish?

Sí. Atendemos a clientes en español. Just let us know your preferred language when you reach out.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express, debit cards, and checks.

What are your hours?

Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Closed weekends. Leave a message after hours and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
