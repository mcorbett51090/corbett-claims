Privacy Policy

Last updated 22 July 2026

This policy covers two separate things: the information you deliberately send us through the appraisal request form, and the small amount of anonymous usage data this website measures. They are handled differently, so they are described separately.

Information you send us through the form

When you submit an appraisal request, you provide details such as your name, the claim number, the vehicle or equipment involved, the date and type of loss, contact details, the loss location, and any photographs or documents you attach.

  • It is used to carry out and support the appraisal you asked for.
  • It is delivered to Corbett Claims over an encrypted connection, and attachments are uploaded directly to secure storage rather than sent as email attachments.
  • It is not sold, rented, or shared for advertising.
  • It may be shared with the parties to your claim where the appraisal requires it — for example an insurer, an attorney, or a repair facility you have asked us to work with.
  • It is kept for as long as needed for the assignment and for the period our professional and legal obligations require.

None of the information you type into the form is sent to our analytics tools. The measurement described below never receives your name, your address, your contact details, your claim number, the free-text description of the loss, or the names of files you attach. The website is built so those values cannot reach an analytics request, rather than relying on us remembering not to send them.

Automated spam submissions are excluded by construction, and those exclusions never involve collecting anything additional about you.

What this website measures

This site uses Google Analytics 4, a service provided by Google, to understand how many people visit, which pages they read, where they arrived from, and how often a visit leads to an appraisal request. We use it to judge whether the site is doing its job — not to identify you.

Google Analytics sets a first-party cookie (named _ga, and one further cookie beginning _ga_) in your browser. Its purpose is to tell a returning visitor apart from a new one so that visits are not double-counted. It does not contain your name or contact details.

Google processes this data on our behalf and as described in its own documentation. For a full explanation of what Google collects through sites that use its services, and how it is used, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

The analytics account belongs to Corbett Claims. The company that builds and maintains this website is granted access to it in order to keep the site working and to report on how it is performing. That access covers the anonymous usage data described here — it does not extend to the appraisal requests you submit, which are delivered directly to Corbett Claims.

We have configured the property so that:

  • Google's advertising features are switched off — this site does not advertise, and no advertising, remarketing, or ad-personalisation signals are enabled.
  • The page address recorded with each visit is stripped of everything except the campaign parameters that tell us which link or search brought you here.
  • Events recorded when a request is submitted carry only the equipment type, assignment type, type of loss, and how many files were delivered — never the contents of any field.

Cookies, and the choice you have

This site shows no cookie banner. That is a deliberate decision for a business serving clients in the United States, not an oversight, and we would rather say so plainly than present a consent dialogue that does nothing.

We do not offer an on-site opt-out toggle, because we would rather not advertise a control we have not built. If you do not want to be measured, you have two effective options that do not depend on us: use your browser's setting to block or clear cookies for this site, or install Google's official Analytics opt-out browser add-on. Blocking analytics does not affect your ability to use this site or to submit an appraisal request.

Other services this site loads

A few third-party services are needed for the site to work: a map provider to show the service area, a font and script host, an address-lookup service used to suggest addresses as you type, a vehicle data lookup, and a bot-protection challenge from Cloudflare. These receive only what they need to answer the request — for example, the partial address you are typing goes to the address-lookup service in order to suggest completions. They are not used for advertising.

Children

This site is intended for business use by adults and is not directed at children.

Changes to this policy

If we add or remove a measurement tool, this page is updated in the same change that makes the difference live, so that what it describes is always what is actually running.
